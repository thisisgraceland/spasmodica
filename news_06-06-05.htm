New Merchandise for June 2005

Jacques Heepoe now available on t-shirts! The silkscreened image of Mr. Heepoe and the text "defy classification" appears in a terrificly understated way on dark brown American Apparel tees. Size discrimination sucks but at this time, these shirts are currently only available in sizes large and small. Just like the Spasmodica doll line, each shirt comes with a detailed character hang tag, as well as a clear vinyl Jacques Heepoe sticker. Sweet!

Oh, yeah, and more shirts are in the works. So if there's a character that you'd love to see on your chest, drop us a line.

Spasmodica's TV Debut on HGTV's Crafters Coast to Coast

Did you catch my episode on HGTV's Crafters Coast to Coast? True to the series, my show shared a glimpse of where and how I make my Spasmodica dolls (namely a Yugison). Even my hubby, Anthony, our doggie, Babbitt, and my niece, Allison, who's the inspiration behind Yugison, all enjoyed a bit of air time.

It was quite a memorable experience letting the HGTV crew take over our house for a day of filming. We even convinced the producers to hang out after the filming was completed (they rule at karaoke!).

The episode originally aired on Monday, May 2, 2005. We'll let you know when it's scheduled for re-broadcast.
